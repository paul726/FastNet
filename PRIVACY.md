# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 7, 2026**

FastNet and FastNet Connect ("the Apps") are developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is handled.

## Data Collection

The Apps do **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- **No analytics or tracking** — no usage data is collected
- **No accounts or registration** — the Apps require no sign-in
- **No network data logging** — traffic passing through the proxy is relayed directly and is never stored, inspected, or recorded
- **No third-party SDKs** — the Apps contain no advertising, analytics, or crash reporting frameworks

## Data Processing

The Apps function as a local network proxy. All network traffic is forwarded in real-time between the local device and the destination server. No data is cached, logged, or retained after transmission.

## Data Storage

The Apps store only user-configurable settings (such as port number) locally on the device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

The Apps do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted to this page.
